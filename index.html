About TuCodec

TuCodec is a company based in Shanghai, which specializes and focuses on video and image codec. We develop advanced algorithms of image segmentation,image compress,etc.We implement highly optimized codes for embedded platforms. We have developped a image codec based on object, it works extreamly well on the high resolution image.

Our team has 50 patents and 20 top papers. Our members are from Nanjing University, Shanghai Jiaotong University, UC Merced, University of Singpore, University of California, Berkeley, ustc. We have PHDs on image processing, pattern recognition, video codec,etc. We want to setup a system that can achieve video analysis and video compression simutaneously.

Technology

Face Parsing

Real-time face labeling under complex face poses and visual patterns. Face labeling is to parse an input face into facial parts, eg. eyes and hair. Our machine learning based algorithm is capable of accurately labeling facial parts under various face pose and complex environment.

In many situation, we want to enhance the people in the image, based on image parsing, we can easily achieve this goal.

Image Segmentation

Image Segmentation is a technology that segments image into many parts and every part is similar inside but different among the others.

Our technology performs 100ms on a PC. We are using this technology to know image better thus we can compress the image better.

Image codec

As the image is booming on the Internet, we develop a machine learning based image codec. Our codec performs 50% better than the JPEG. Also, our codec works well on a low bit rate. Our technology can be used on game image compression, social networks image compression, VR/AR image compression, video independent frame compression, etc. Our codec performs 200ms on an iphone6(if you need more compression rate, more compute resource is needed).

Our codec get a shape of the image, we performs better on image scaling than JPEG. Also, our image can decode a part of the image. And retrive the object without decoding the whole image.

Object Tracking

Visual object tracking is a technology to recover the trajectories of respective objects in a given video. It plays a key role in many intelligent visual systems, such as video surveillance, identity recognition, and behavior recognition.

We use video object tracking on the video compression and video analysis. As we segment the object before, we track the object and compress the object.

Image Stiching

Image Stiching is a technology that stiches a set of image and get a whole image for the scence.

We develop a real time image stiching method that used for video scence reconstruction. Once we get a whole map of the scence, we can perform video compression better. Using our video codec, you can easily get a sanp of the video without decoding the whole video.

Video Codec

As MPEG4 is abolished in 1998, object based codec haven't been full researched for 20 years. Object based video coding is perfect for AR/VR because of the easy interation.

The image understanding has a giant pace on the last 5 years. We are trying to develop a object based video compression codec for the high resolution (like 4K,8K, etc). Now, we are still working on this!
